About Us

Thinking the way forward

Swarnatara Group established in year 2006 is a conglomerate with diverse business interests and operations across multiple countries. With a Pan-India presence, we cater to clientele seeking the best-in-class financial services through our flagship company, Swarnatara Consultancy Trading Private Limited.

It is our staunch belief that Innovation is the key to sustenance and that one facet of Innovation is doing new things. Following this mantra, in the last 4-5 years we have set up various group companies including Swarnatara Financial Holding Markets Pvt. Ltd. that specializes in trading of CFDs, Forex and Gold. We have also forayed into retail through our ventures, Swarnatara Diamond and Jewels Pvt. Ltd. and Swarnatara Fashions Pvt.Ltd.

The Group takes immense pride in the business and has an edge over similar such market players due to our exceptional knowledge and depth of expertise. The goal is to empower investors with knowledge, requisite tools and sound advice at low costs thus ensuring good returns. Investments are all about future and trust is the main component that binds all investments with us.
