Management

The Board of directors of Swarnatara Group have a strong commitment for accelerated growth and to provide value to their clientele. This is achieved through focussed innovation and an unwavering vision with clarity in thought and action. Mr. Venkatrenga Gupta is ably assisted by the directors Ms. Kavitha Shakthi S. and Mr. Hariharan D. K. in the quest for transforming our vision into reality.

The team of professional experts constitute various departments including Administration, Accounts, and Marketing and play a vital role in the day-to-day functioning of each of our Companies. The Administration team is the backbone of the company. This team is the link between various departments and ensures the smooth flow of information across the company.

The Accounts team provides the necessary accounting services and financial support to the various departments as needed. Like any other company, they take care of the Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivables, Payroll, Fixed Assets and other financial matters. The Marketing team is the face of our various group companies and has the primary responsibility to reach out to prospects, customers, investors and the community, thus creating an image that projects the company and its vision in the right manner.

We staunchly believe that we exist because of our customers. The customer facing team at each of the group companies is trained to handle the needs of each customer and to deal with various situations. It is our constant endeavour that clients remain with us for the long haul and we strive to not only achieve Customer Satisfaction but to attain Customer Delight.

To achieve success, a company needs to pay attention to their Human Resources. The employees of a company are their biggest asset and by focussing on their needs, the company has a higher probability of success. The Human Resources team fully understands this and is constantly focussed on devising policies and activities that keeps the employees motivated and aligned towards the shared vision and goals of the company.

Founder Director

Swarnatara Group’s driving force is its people – the visionaries, believers and experts who have helped build a team envisaging a successful future journey.

Our founder Director Mr. Venkatrenga Gupta is a visionary whose tireless efforts and innovative thinking has made Swarnatara Group a force to reckon with. His intuitive and strong decision making ability along with astute leadership and passion for excellence has helped us diversify across industry verticals in a short span of time.

He is a pioneer and his depth of expertise in the realm of online trading is one of the best in the industry. He has toiled for years to bring to investors and traders the advance signal functionality that has benefitted many. He has been instrumental in fine tuning indicators and his trading win ratio is exceptionally high.

Mr. Venkatrenga’s ability to think ahead of the competition has led to the group companies growing from strength to strength, on the path to become market leaders in their respective fields.
