Investors Desk

As an organization that is on a growth trajectory, the various group companies are always on the lookout for investments and like-minded partners who can be part of our growth journey. These investments could be in the form of finance, skills and knowledge or leveraging your network.

  • Swarnatara Consultancy Trading Pvt. Ltd.

    SCTPL has a Pan India presence with a focus on online trading. If you are a financial institution or an individual investor who believes that you can add value and at the same time have access to larger pool of resources and higher benefits, do get in touch with us. Quick Contact

  • SFH Markets Pty. Ltd.

    SFH is looking for investors both in India and locations abroad with expertise in the financial services domain specific to Forex, CFDs and Gold to partner with us and expand to various geographies across the globe. Quick Contact

  • Swarnatara Diamonds and Jewels Pvt. Ltd.

    Swarnatara Diamonds focusses on custom made jewellery using heart and arrow diamonds that are of superior quality. We are keen on partnering with diamond merchants, businessmen or jewellery designers who are looking to establish / grow their business in this field leading to a mutually beneficial association. Quick Contact

  • Swarnatara Health Care Resorts Pvt. Ltd.

    Through this venture we aim to revolutionize the health and wellness industry in India and would love to have like-minded investors / healthcare experts partner with us. If you share this vision and have the required resources and skills to partner with us, we are just a call away. Quick Contact

  • Swarnatara Fashions Pvt. Ltd.

    Swarnatara Fashions is looking for partners who are passionate about the Fashion Industry and are excited to showcase their skills and be part of the growth journey. Do get in touch with us today and let’s together revolutionize the fashion industry. Quick Contact

  • Swarnatara Developers Pvt. Ltd.

    The Real Estate arm of the Swarnatara Group is looking to make rapid inroads into the Construction Industry. With this goal in mind, we are looking for partners, investors, interior designers and land owners who are looking for developers to launch their projects. Do get in touch with us soon to establish a mutually beneficial association. Quick Contact

  • Swarnatara Spirituals

    Swarnatara Spirituals has been conceived with the objective of providing seekers with dedicated services and products, including Astrology, Numerology, Match Making and Vastu that aim to bring happiness, peace, stability and wealth to our clientele. Do get in touch with us. Quick Contact

  • Swarnatara Tours and Travels PVT LTD

    Seeing the world through STT is a dream come true. Understanding your feelings and delivering tailor made packages for the right event and moment is the core strength of STT. Come with us for a world class experience, you will be Home away Home. . Quick Contact

  • Swarnatara Collaborations PVT LTD

    Bringing the right product, direct to home from the farms of India and Collaborating with the manufacturers of high end products for our day to day requirements.
