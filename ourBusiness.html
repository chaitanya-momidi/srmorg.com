Our Business

Swarnatara Group currently operates businesses pan India. We incorporate cutting-edge innovation and aspire to have a robust operational model across our group companies for overall business excellence.

Swarnatara Consultancy Trading Private Limited (SCTPL) offers financial services, specifically focusing on investment advisory and financial management. The product range offered include Equities, Derivatives, Commodities, Currency and Mutual Funds. SCTPL Advisory Services is a large gambit of services including Trade Advisory, Investment Advisory, Financial Advisory, Fund Management, Portfolio Management, Client Account Management, Asset Management, Brokerage Services and Training Programmes.
